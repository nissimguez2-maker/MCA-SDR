# Outbound Strategist Audit: MCA SDR Operating System

**Lane:** Merchant buying signals, falsifiable ICP, warm-prospect concentration by lead-source tier.

## Market reality

UCC-1 filings are the highest-confidence cold signal: a lien proves the merchant already qualified for MCA. A business 60-90 days into a 4-6 month term is near payoff. Aged-lead vendors resell each file to 8-12 brokers. Signal personalization determines who closes.

Falsifiable ICP: US-based, 1-20 employees, $10-100k/month, 12+ months TIB, card/ACH industries (restaurants, trucking, HVAC, retail, auto repair, salons). Disqualifiers: under 6 months TIB, under $8k/month, 3+ active positions, cannabis/firearms/adult, federal tax lien over $50k.

Signal tiers: Tier 1 (act within 24 hours): UCC-1 from a known MCA funder filed 60-150 days ago; prior funded merchant silent 4-6 months; inbound form with callback. Tier 2 (72 hours): LinkedIn growth or equipment post; Google Business showing a 12-18 month-old business just opening. Tier 3: aged leads 6-24 months; UCC over 9 months old.

## What the market needs

Signal-based outreach converts 3-5x over generic cold. The plan has the correct lead-source priority order but no signal layer on sources 2 and 3. Without a trigger, warm re-engagement is a cold call with better contact data.

## Opportunities to seize

1. **UCC self-sourcing as primary cold channel.** State SOS portals are free in TX, FL, NY, CA; vendors cost $50-100/month. Filter for known MCA funders filed 60-150 days ago. Cap at 10-15 contacts per day. Estimated 2-4x lift over generic aged lists.
2. **Warm re-engagement with a trigger hook.** Aged leads 90-180 days old expressed intent. A 20-30 lead sequence per week outpaces fresh sourcing at zero incremental cost.
3. **LinkedIn as signal monitor.** Target owners in restaurants, trucking, and trades posting about growth or hiring in the past 30 days. Drive to the qual page; signal-triggered reply rates run 8-12% vs. under 1% for cold pitches.
4. **Referral ask at every funded deal.** Two minutes at funding produces the warmest inbound. Skipped at volume shops and cannot be automated.

## Hard truths and risks

1. **UCC payoff timing is estimated.** Filings show funder and date, not balance. Build a filing-date-plus-typical-term model before dialing; calling mid-advance burns the contact.
2. **Company lead dependency is fragile.** If flow is thin the plan stalls. UCC self-sourcing must run in parallel from week one.
3. **Stacking is invisible at outreach time.** Second and third positions surface only in bank statements. Avoid targeting merchants with 3+ visible MCA liens.

## Facts to verify

- MCA funder names most common on UCC-1s in TX, FL, NY, CA (builds the filter list).
- Liens.com and SearchIQ pricing at under $100/month.
- TX HB 700 broker disclosure: signed or in committee as of June 2026.
- TCPA written consent for texting UCC-sourced contacts under the January 2025 FCC one-to-one rule.
