# Search Query Analyst Audit

**Lane:** Query intent and language SMB owners use when seeking funding, as intent intelligence for the qualification page, opener copy, and lead scoring.

## Market Reality

Transactional queries with urgency modifiers are the fundable signal: "same day business funding," "merchant cash advance bad credit," "MCA no credit check." These merchants compare speed, not price. Informational ("what is a merchant cash advance") are researchers, rarely fundable in 30 days. Distress queries are hard negatives: "how to get out of merchant cash advance," "MCA debt relief," "MCA stacking lawsuit." One modifier shifts class: "fast" moves commercial to transactional; "relief" flips it to distress. Top-value unigrams: fast, bad credit, today. Top-risk: relief, lawsuit, scam.

## What the Market Needs

The page must mirror transactional language in the first sentence or contacts bounce. No disqualifier exists for contacts in MCA default. Urgency signals in free-text fields go unscored.

## Opportunities to Seize

1. **Mirror transactional language in headline and first CTA.** "Fast approval," "bad credit OK," "same-day funding possible" match the words that prompted the click.
2. **Add a distress disqualifier at form step two.** "Do you have an existing MCA you cannot repay?" routes yes answers off the booking path before any call time is spent.
3. **Score urgency n-grams in free-text inputs.** Payroll, urgent, this week, overdue outweigh any structured field as a warmth signal.
4. **Reserve informational copy for nurture.** Factor-rate education belongs in a follow-up, not the headline.

## Hard Truths and Risks

1. **"Bad credit OK" pulls merchants who still cannot qualify.** Revenue and time-in-business gates must fire before the booking link or calls burn on unfundable files.
2. **Distress volume is growing post-2022.** Without a hard form gate, outbound campaigns route defaulting borrowers into finite daily capacity.
3. **Inferred query intent does not equal deal intent.** Warmth scoring must weight on-page behavior, not query language inferred from the outbound message.

## Facts to Verify

- Volume and trend for "merchant cash advance bad credit" and "same day business funding" (Semrush/Ahrefs, 2025-2026).
- Growth rate of "MCA debt relief" versus 2022 baseline.
- Whether "same day funding" or "bad credit OK" as page copy triggers CA SB 1235 or NY RISA disclosure.

## Cross-Lane Flags

- **Copywriter/messaging:** transactional query strings drive headline and CTA copy; distress n-gram list drives what to avoid.
- **Lead scoring/ops:** distress n-gram matching is a hard-negative gate. "MCA relief" in free text scores zero regardless of other signals.
- **Compliance:** "same day funding" and "bad credit OK" must clear state commercial financing disclosure rules before launch.
